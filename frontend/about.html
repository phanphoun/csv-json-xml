About PNC Student Management System

Welcome to the PNC Student Management System, a comprehensive solution designed to streamline student record management at Passerelles Numériques Cambodia (PNC).

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an efficient, user-friendly platform for managing student records, enabling administrators to focus on what matters most - supporting student success.

Key Features

  • Efficient student record management
  • Real-time data access and updates
  • Secure data storage and backup
  • Easy data export in multiple formats
  • Responsive design for all devices

This system was developed as part of our commitment to leveraging technology for educational excellence at PNC. We believe in empowering our staff with the best tools to support our students' journey.
